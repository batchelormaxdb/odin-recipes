Recipe for Lasagna

A tasty, easy to make lasagna recipe

This recipe requires very few ingredients and will feed your whole family!

Just follow the ingredients listed below and the rest will be a piece of... um... well, lasagna.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Spaghetti Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Pasta

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Cook ground beef until brown.
  3. Lay down one quarter of the pasta in a flat layer.
  4. Lay down one quarter of the ground beef over the pasta.
  5. Lay down cheese and spaghetti sauce over the ground beef.
  6. Repeat steps 3-5 until all ingredients are gone.
  7. Bake result for 20-25 minutes and enjoy!
