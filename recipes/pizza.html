Recipe for pizza

A tasty, easy to make pizza recipe

This recipe requires very few ingredients and will feed your whole family!

Just follow the ingredients listed below and the rest will be a piece of... um... well, pizza.

Ingredients

  • Pepperoni
  • Marinara Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Pizza Dough

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees.
  2. Roll out pizza dough on a flour covered surface.
  3. Spread out sauce onto dough evenly.
  4. Sprinkle on as much cheese as you like!
  5. Lay out the Pepperoni.
  6. Bake result for 18-20 minutes and enjoy!
