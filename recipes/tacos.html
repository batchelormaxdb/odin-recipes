Recipe for tacos

A tasty, easy to make tacos recipe

This recipe requires very few ingredients and will feed your whole family!

Follow the recipe below to make some mouth watering tacos!

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Taco Seasoning
  • Cheese
  • Corn Tortillas

Steps

  1. Lay pan onto stove on medium heat.
  2. Cook ground beef until brown.
  3. Add water and taco seasoning to beef and stir regularly.
  4. In another pan, lay out a tortilla on some vegetable oil.
  5. Salt and dress tortilla with any toppings you desire.
  6. Enjoy!
